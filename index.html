How to use

The application checks all the GDE's Google+ public stream (posts shared Public or with public Communities) searching for posts that contain the hashtag #gde.

To be processed by the application, other information are required, so GDE's need to add additional tags to the Google+ post they are sharing to specify the kind of content they are sharing.


The special # tags are divided in 2 categories:

  • Product Tags

  • Activity Type Tags

Following a cheat sheet of the Tags:


Product Tags

#admob AdMob

#googledrive Google Drive SDK
#glass Google Glass

#googleplus Google+ Platform

#uxdesign UX & Design
Activity Type Tags
#bugreport BugReports: Report Bug on Google APIs
#article Article: Write technical article about use cases, tips & tricks, particulare use and experience using an API
#blogpost BlogPost: Maybe less technical than Articles, less formal, but still informative and helpful informations for Developers
#book Book: A full book about a technology, best practices, examples, guides, etc... all printed on Paper (or digital)
#techdocs Technical Documentation: Help Improve the documentation on Google Open Source Projects
#translation Translation: Many articles are in English, but Developers are all around the world and it's helpful if content is translated in their native language
#techtalk Tech Talks: Not Scared of the stage? Talk at conventions, GDGs and MeetUps and present technologies to Developers in front of you
#opensourcecode Open Source Code: Got a cool library/application and want to share it because can help other developer? Open source it and share with the World
#forumpost Forum Posts: Help answering question directly in Forums and StackOverflow
#community Communty: Engage with the community live
#video Videos: Presentations and tutorials presented with a video are more engaging and help convey the passion we feel about the technology
#tutorial Tutorials: Lead the developer step by step during his journey for a better knowledge of the technology

Who are the Google Developer Experts?

The Google Developer Experts (GDE) title recognizes the outstanding developers who exhibit great expertise in one or more Google technologies and share it with the developer community.

GDEs are gurus, mentors and evangelists; they create tutorials, code samples, and write books and blogs; they publish videos, drive community projects, and present at events large and small. These independent developers bring their real-world experience and knowledge working with Google technologies to developer communities worldwide.
